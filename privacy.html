Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 July 2026  ·  Last Updated: 1 July 2026

Contents

Section 01

Overview

IDCraft Pro is a 100% offline Windows desktop application. It does not connect to the internet, does not transmit your data to any server, and does not require an account. Your employee data, photos, and generated ID cards never leave your device.

This Privacy Policy explains what limited information is collected through our marketing website (the "Website") at piyush-2103.github.io, and how it is handled. This policy does not apply to the desktop application itself, which collects no data whatsoever.

By using our Website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Website.

Section 02

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the Website and respond to enquiries.

Information you provide voluntarily:

  • Contact form submissions — your name, email address, subject, and message content when you submit the contact form.
  • Review submissions — your name, optional role or organisation, star rating, and written review text when you choose to leave a review on the Website.

Information collected automatically:

  • Basic browser storage data (localStorage) used solely to remember your light/dark theme preference across visits. This is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to us.
  • Standard server access logs (IP address, browser type, page visited, timestamp) may be recorded by GitHub Pages, our hosting provider. We do not access or process these logs beyond what GitHub Pages retains as part of its standard operations.

We do not collect payment information, identity documents, biometric data, or any data entered into or generated by the IDCraft Pro desktop application.

Section 03

How We Use Information

Information collected through the Website is used exclusively for:

  • Responding to enquiries — contact form submissions are used solely to reply to your message. We do not add you to any mailing list without explicit consent.
  • Displaying reviews — review submissions are stored via JSONBin.io and displayed publicly on the Website. By submitting a review, you consent to your name, role, and review text being displayed publicly.
  • Improving the Website — aggregate, anonymised usage patterns may be used to improve the Website's content and user experience.
  • Licence management — if you purchase a licence, your email address is used to deliver your licence key. It is not used for marketing without your explicit consent.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Section 04

Data Storage

Desktop application: All data processed by the IDCraft Pro desktop application — including employee names, photos, Excel files, and generated ID cards — is stored exclusively on your local Windows device. Arham Studios has no access to this data under any circumstances.

Contact form submissions: Form submissions are handled by Web3Forms and/or FormSubmit.co and delivered to our email address. We retain this correspondence for up to 12 months for support purposes, after which it is deleted.

Reviews: Review data (name, role, rating, review text, submission date) is stored on JSONBin.io, a third-party JSON storage service. Reviews are stored publicly and are accessible to anyone with the API endpoint. Do not include personal or sensitive information in your review.

Theme preference: Your light/dark mode preference is stored in your browser's localStorage under the key idcraft-theme. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

Section 05

Third-Party Services

The Website uses a limited number of third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

  • GitHub Pages — Website hosting. May collect standard server access logs. GitHub Privacy Statement.
  • Google Fonts — Serves Nunito, IBM Plex Mono, and Bebas Neue typefaces. May log font requests including your IP address. Google Privacy Policy.
  • JSONBin.io — Stores and retrieves public review data. JSONBin Privacy Policy.
  • Web3Forms / FormSubmit.co — Processes contact form submissions. Your form data passes through their servers en route to our inbox.
  • YouTube — Embedded video demos are loaded using YouTube's privacy-enhanced mode (youtube-nocookie.com). YouTube may still collect data when you interact with embedded videos.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

Section 06

Cookies

The IDCraft Pro Website does not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics.

The only browser storage we use is localStorage to remember your theme preference (idcraft-theme: "light" or "dark"). This is a functional preference, not a tracking mechanism, and it is stored entirely on your device.

Third-party services embedded in the Website (Google Fonts, YouTube) may set their own cookies in accordance with their privacy policies. You can manage or block these through your browser's privacy settings.

Section 07

Children's Privacy

IDCraft Pro and this Website are intended for use by businesses, institutions, and professionals. They are not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in jurisdictions where a higher age threshold applies).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through our Website, please contact us immediately at idcraftp@gmail.com and we will delete that information promptly.

Section 08

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect information submitted through the Website. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Specific measures we take include:

  • The Website is served entirely over HTTPS via GitHub Pages.
  • Contact form submissions are transmitted securely to our email provider.
  • We do not store payment card information — all payments are processed by UPI or third-party payment processors.
  • The desktop application processes all data locally and has no network access, eliminating remote data-breach risk entirely for application users.

Please be aware that review submissions are stored in a publicly accessible JSON store. Do not include passwords, financial information, or other sensitive personal data in your review.

Section 09

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data, including any reviews you have submitted and contact form correspondence we retain.
  • Right to restriction — request that we restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to object — object to processing of your data where we rely on legitimate interests as a legal basis.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at idcraftp@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests.

Section 10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, the Website, or applicable law. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the Website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

For significant changes that materially affect your rights, we will make reasonable efforts to notify users through a prominent notice on the Website.
