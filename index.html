03 — Capabilities

What It Does

A complete toolkit for enterprise-scale ID generation. No cloud. No subscriptions. No complexity.

01
Excel Data Binding
Import any .xlsx sheet and map columns to template fields. Hundreds of records processed in a single click.
02
Auto QR & Barcode
Dynamically bind any Excel column to generate unique QR codes and Data Matrix codes per card, automatically.
03
Photo Auto-Match
Name-match photo folders to Excel rows automatically. Bind 500+ employee photos with zero manual assignment.
04
Dual-Sided Printing
Design front and back faces independently. Merge into print-ready A4 grids with custom spacing and rotation.
05
Multi-Format Export
Export in JPEG, lossless PNG, high-res PDF, or vector SVG. All formats print-ready at your configured DPI.
06
Fully Offline
Zero cloud upload. All sensitive employee data, photos, and templates remain on your local Windows machine.
